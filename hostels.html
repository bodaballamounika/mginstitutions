Hostels Facilities

Apart from providing exceptional academics,M&G has always believed in keeping the hostel environments safe, hygienic and refreshing for the students. In an effort to making the hostel rooms more pleasant,M&G has recently introduced uniformly printed bed linen at some of the hostels on an experimental basis. Each student was given 3 Bed Sheets, 2 Cover Sheets, 3 Pillow Covers and 1 Fleece Blanket. Uniformly themed window curtains were provided to each room along with a Shoe Racks, Cup Boards and Dry Cloth Stands. The mattresses provided to students are Health Treated to keep them safe from bugs and bacteria. Elated by the huge positive response from the students who were provided with these facilities, we are extending the same to other hostels gradually.

M&GInstitutions provides separate hostels for boys and girls situated within the hospital campus.
